Methodology

How It Works

Three phases. One score. Zero guesswork.

01 / Audit

Audit

We run structured test cases across your tagging, alerting, allocation model, and tooling. Every test produces a Pass, Fail, or Warning result.

02 / Validate

Validate

We calculate your FinOps Defect Score (0–100) from objective test results. We benchmark it against the CFCMM maturity model and your industry peer group.

03 / Govern

Govern

You receive a prioritised Defect Register with financial impact per finding, remediation roadmap, and acceptance criteria. Optional retainer keeps the score trending up.
