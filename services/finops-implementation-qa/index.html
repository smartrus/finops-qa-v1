Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with the implementation vendor during the QA?

We co-design acceptance criteria with the vendor at the start. After that, testing is fully independent. Defects found are attributed to the vendor in the defect register, but our role is to serve the client — not to arbitrate between client and vendor.

What if the implementation has significant defects?

We document every defect with evidence, severity classification, and financial impact. The client decides what remediation to require from the vendor. We can re-test after remediation and issue the Certificate of Acceptance when criteria are met.
