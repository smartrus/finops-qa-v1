Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is spend-weighted tagging coverage and why does it matter?

Resource-count coverage measures what percentage of your resources are tagged. Spend-weighted coverage measures what percentage of your actual cloud bill is attributable to a valid team or cost centre. A single untagged NAT Gateway at $8,000/month is 0.01% of resources but potentially your entire allocation error.

Do you remediate the tagging gaps, or just report them?

Both. We deliver tag remediation scripts in your preferred format (AWS Tag Editor, Python/boto3, or Terraform). We also validate that the remediation closed the identified gap.
